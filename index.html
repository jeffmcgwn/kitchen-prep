Meat & Fish

CHICKEN TENDERS

What you need

  • Cutting Board
  • Mallet
  • Knife
  • Plastic Wrap
  • Portion Bags
  • Scale
  • Third Pan (Deep)

What To Do

  • Cover the cutting board with tenders
  • Cover the tenders with plastic wrap
  • Pound out the tenders to flatten them
  • If the tenders are exceptionally large, slice off the ends and save them for line toppings
  • Weigh out 3/4 lb of tenders
  • Put tenders in a portion bag and put the bags in the pan

What you need

COLD BREW

What you need

  • Cold brew barrel
  • 7 bags of coffee
  • White filter
  • Filter bag

What To Do

  • Place small white filter at bottom of white bucket near the tap/pourer
  • Fill with water to the prefill line
  • Put filter bag in the smaller black bucket
  • Add 7 bags of coffee (1lb bags)
  • Fill with water to the max fill line
  • Put cover on, let sit in the cooler by 3rd window overnight
  • Pick up black bucket using orange handles and turn it to let it drain for 30 minutes
